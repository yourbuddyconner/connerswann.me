Beautiful Containers

Containers are a wonderful thing, allowing a system to be abstracted away to simply the resources it requires. At O(1) Labs, we recognize the power of this paradigm, and strive to treat the container as a "first class citizen" in our infrastructure. While certain applications, like Block Production, aren't necessarily well-suited to running inside potentially short-lived containers, there's plenty of other applications that could benefit from this sort of workload orchestration.
